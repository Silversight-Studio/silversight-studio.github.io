Our Four Core Principles

  • Respect: Treat everyone with dignity; no bullying or harassment.
  • Safety: Protect users from harm, threats, or exploitation.
  • Privacy: Share only content you have the right to share.
  • Authenticity: No impersonation, manipulated media, or spam.

Prohibited Content & Behavior

The following are not allowed. Violations may result in content removal, suspension, or account termination.

  • Harassment & Hate: Threats, slurs, or content that dehumanizes protected groups.
  • Sexual Content & Nudity: Explicit sexual acts, non‑consensual imagery, sexual exploitation of minors.
  • Violence & Illegal Activities: Graphic violence, instructions to commit crimes, promotion of weapons for harm.
  • Self‑Harm: Content that encourages or depicts self-injury or suicide.
  • Child Safety: Any sexualized content involving minors or endangering child welfare.
  • Intellectual Property Infringement: Unauthorized use of copyrighted or trademarked works.
  • Misinformation & Fraud: Content that deliberately deceives, scams, or spreads false information that could cause harm.
  • Spam & Platform Abuse: Bulk unsolicited content, inauthentic engagement, or manipulation of platform metrics.

Enforcement Approach

We use a mix of automated systems and human review to detect violations. Actions we may take include:

  • Warning notices
  • Content removal
  • Feature restrictions (e.g., posting limits)
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent account termination

Severity, context, and user history influence enforcement.

Reporting & Appeals

If you see content that violates these guidelines, please:

  1. Tap the • • • menu next to the post.
  2. Select Report and choose the reason.

We review most reports within 24 hours. If you believe your content was removed in error, you can appeal via support@silversightllc.com.
