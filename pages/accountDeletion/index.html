            <h1>Account Deletion</h1>

            <p class="effective-date">How to delete your RivalMe account</p>

        <p class="intro">You can request deletion of your RivalMe account and associated data at any time. This page explains the available methods and what happens when your account is deleted.</p>


        <h2>Delete Your Account In-App</h2>

        <p>The fastest way to delete your account is directly within the RivalMe app:</p>

        

        <div class="steps-box">

            <ol>

                <li>Open the <strong>RivalMe</strong> app</li>

                <li>Go to your <strong>Profile</strong> tab</li>

                <li>Tap <strong>Settings</strong> (gear icon)</li>

                <li>Scroll down and tap <strong>Delete Account</strong></li>

                <li>Follow the confirmation prompts</li>

            </ol>

        </div>


        <p>Your account will be deleted immediately upon confirmation.</p>


        <h2>Request Deletion via Email</h2>

        <p>If you cannot access the app (e.g., you no longer have the device or cannot log in), you can request account deletion by email:</p>

        

        <div class="contact-box">

            <p><strong>Email:</strong> <a href="mailto:support@silversightllc.com?subject=Account%20Deletion%20Request">support@silversightllc.com</a></p>

            <p><strong>Subject:</strong> Account Deletion Request</p>

            <p><strong>Include:</strong> The email address associated with your RivalMe account</p>

        </div>


        <p>Email requests are typically processed within <strong>30 days</strong>. We may contact you to verify your identity before processing the deletion.</p>


        <h2>What Gets Deleted</h2>

        <p>When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:</p>

        <ul>

            <li>Your profile information (username, bio, profile photo)</li>

            <li>All posts, photos, and videos you uploaded</li>

            <li>All comments and likes you made</li>

            <li>Your contest participation history</li>

            <li>Earnings history and wallet balance</li>

            <li>Notification preferences and settings</li>

        </ul>


        <div class="warning-box">

            <p><strong>Warning:</strong> Account deletion is <strong>permanent and cannot be undone</strong>. If you have unreleased earnings in your wallet, those will be forfeited. Please withdraw any available balance before deleting your account.</p>

        </div>


        <h2>Data Retention</h2>

        <p>Some minimal data may be retained after account deletion as required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as:</p>

        <ul>

            <li>Transaction records for tax and legal compliance</li>

            <li>Records necessary to resolve disputes or enforce our terms</li>

            <li>Anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you</li>

        </ul>
